About

“BW” is a footwear brand that seamlessly blends athletic performance with a regal aesthetic. The brand is a fusion of Brazilian energy and sophisticated style. The logo even features a crown.

Our shoes are engineered for comfort and performance. Featuring black and gold colours together, they inspire royal heritage and ensure that every step you take is one of power and grace.

“BW” is for those who demand excellence in both form and function. More than just a shoe, it is a symbol of a lifestyle that is both active and luxurious. Step into a world where every stride is a testament to your inner royalty.
